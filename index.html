About Me

Avery Dews

Mindful, Inquisitive, Creative.

I am a recent graduate of the University of Michigan, earning my Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science in May 2016. Interested in data science, software development, and social justice, I am currently exploring entry level opportunities in technology field.

I am a native Metro Detroiter and cat lover. In addition to studying Computer Science, I am also a graduate of the Screen Arts and Cultures department, having completed the Global Media Studies minor with a focus on 1960s art film and early cable television. My hobbies include collecting vinyl records, photography, and spending copious hours reading Wikipedia. Recently, I have begun learning how to work with clay with the Ceramics Club at the University of Michigan.

Learning

One of my goals is to continue to learn and become proficient in other programming languages. Within the next year, I plan to practice coding in Java, JavaScript, and Swift. In addition to this, I plan to enhance my developing skills by contributing to open-source projects, as well as create some projects of my own.

Career

My degree and background are in Computer Science, and while I was a student I created multiple applications and programs in object-oriented languages like C++. I also gained very valuable experience in customer service while being a Resident Advisor. My ideal entry-level position would allow me to utilize and develop those skills, and that lead me to look into product support roles.


My goal is to work my way up to a more senior software development role after a few years of experiencing product support, and my ultimate goal is a senior software consultant role.


Financial

Within the next year, I plan to establish my retirement fund. I plan to try to save 20% of my income to contribute to this fund as well as a fund to purchase a house, which I hope to do at the end of my 5 year plan.

Spirituality

In addition to my physical and mental health, my plans for the next 5 years also include a new focus on my spirituality. Within the next year, I want to start meditating on a regular basis as well as journaling.


Health

My ideal plan for my health for the next five years is to be balanced. I ultimately want to establish a much more healthy pattern of eating and exercise. I currently jog several times a week, as a former cross country runner, but I would like to diversify my physical activities by getting back into playing organized soccer, swimming, and weight training.


My goal is to start losing weight gradually within the next year, and maintain a healthy BMI.

Recreation

One of the things that I'd like to experience that I haven't been able to is travel, and a large part of my plan for the next 5 years is to go to some of the places that have intrigued me the most. In 2018, two years from now, my goal is to plan and take a trip to Europe. At this point the places I'd like to visit the most are Germany, the UK, Bosnia, Croatia, and Serbia. In 2019, my goal is to take another trip to the Phillipines and visit the village where my grandmother grew up.


Outside of travelling, I plan to continue reading about and watching independent and foreign films, attempting to be a better photographer, learning more about modern art (abstract expressionism is my favorite movement,) and getting better at working with clay. My goal is to find and join a studio where I can continue making ceramics. I'd like to make my own set of dishware.


One of my more loftier goals is to run a marathon in four years.


Relocation

I've spent the majority of my life in the Midwest, and while I have enjoyed it, one of the things I'd like to do most in the next five years is live in a different region of the United States.


One of my goals that I want to work toward in the next year is to move to the Bay Area and work there. I plan to stay there for at least three years.


During the fourth year, I want to plan a move back to the Midwest, probably to Chicago or Minneappolis, and excecute that move by the end of the fifth year.


Relationships

I really value the relationships I've created with people I've met while attending the University of Michigan. Specifically, I consider the close friends I made while being a Resident Advisor to be family.


One of my goals on the aspect of my personal relationships is to remain close with people. Although we are all recent graduates, I plan to visit them all once a year, wherever they live.


My next goal is pretty simple, I want to call my parents at least once a week.


Fun

Now that I've finished my undergradute degree, I have a few plans for things that didn't fall into the previous categories.


After I got my ears pierced in 2014, I thought about getting a tattoo. Since then, I decided that one of my personal goals is to get a solid armband tattoo in the next year. I'm not sure what the exact design will be, however.


As I mentioned earlier, I am a total cat person. I grew up with cats in my house, so I would like to adopt one within the next two years, after I settle down someplace.
